# Product Guidelines

## 1. Prose Style

All explanatory text, labels, and documentation within the NeuroSolve application should adhere to an **academic and formal** prose style.

*   **Language:** Use precise and unambiguous technical language.
*   **Tone:** The tone should be authoritative and informative, similar to that of a textbook or a research paper.
*   **Clarity:** Prioritize clarity and correctness in all explanations. Avoid slang, colloquialisms, and overly conversational language.

## 2. Visual Identity

The user interface of NeuroSolve should be **clean and minimalist**.

*   **Layout:** The layout should be uncluttered and intuitive, with a strong focus on the logical flow of information.
*   **Focus:** The primary focus should be on the data, visualizations, and the step-by-step algorithmic output. UI elements should support this focus, not distract from it.
*   **Color Palette:** Use a simple and professional color palette. Avoid excessive use of bright colors.
