# Product Guide: NeuroSolve

## 1. Overview

NeuroSolve is a Python application for numerical and symbolic computation, designed for educational purposes. It aims to make complex mathematical methods transparent and understandable by not just providing solutions, but by showing the step-by-step process and explaining the underlying concepts.

## 2. Target Audience

The primary users of NeuroSolve are:

*   **University Students:** Students in mathematics, computer science, and engineering courses who are learning about numerical methods.
*   **Educators and Instructors:** Teachers and professors who need a tool to visually demonstrate how these algorithms work in a classroom setting.

## 3. Goals

The main objectives of NeuroSolve are:

*   **Problem Solving:** To provide a functional tool for solving common numerical problems, including root finding, linear algebra, and integration.
*   **Algorithmic Visualization:** To offer a clear, step-by-step visualization of the iterations and processes within the numerical algorithms.
*   **Conceptual Explanation:** To explain the mathematical principles and logic behind the implemented methods, helping users understand *why* the algorithms work the way they do.

## 4. Core Features

The initial version of NeuroSolve will focus on the following features:

*   **Step-by-Step Logging:** Detailed, iterative logging for all implemented solvers (Newton-Raphson, Gaussian Elimination, Trapezoidal Rule).
*   **Symbolic Mathematics:** Integration with SymPy to provide exact derivatives and integrals, complementing the numerical methods.
*   **AI Analyst:** An integrated assistant that can explain the mathematical steps and concepts to the user on demand.
